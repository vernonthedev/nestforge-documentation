## What this site covers

This documentation is built directly around the current `nestforge/` workspace:

- the public `nestforge` crate and its re-exports
- the supporting runtime crates such as `nestforge-http`, `nestforge-config`, `nestforge-graphql`, and `nestforge-grpc`
- the CLI in `crates/nestforge-cli`
- the example applications under `examples/`
- the contributor workflow described in `CONTRIBUTING.md`

The goal is to serve two different audiences without forcing them through the same
reading order:

- application teams who need to ship services with NestForge
- framework contributors who need to extend, test, or release the workspace safely
